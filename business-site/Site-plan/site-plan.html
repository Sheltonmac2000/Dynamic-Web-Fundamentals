Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to advertise an accounting and bookkeeping services company. It is primarly for exposure of this company on the market.

Audience

THe audience of this website is potential clients who would benegit from the services which includes accounting and bookkeeping, taxes and other fincancial services.

Dynamic elements

Some of the dynamic elements are:

  • A Dynamic contact Form with a dropdown menu
  • Some animations/
  • A function to toggle the menu button to show or hide the navbar on smaller screens. When the screen is at least 600px wide, the navbar is displayed, but when it is smaller, then the navbar is automatically hidden, but the user can toggle it on and off, by clicking the menu button.
